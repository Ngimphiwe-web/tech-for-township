Top Side Hustles to Start Today

1. Online Surveys

Earn airtime and cash by answering questions on apps like TGM Panel, MOBROG, etc.

2. Tutoring Kids

Use your matric skills to help kids in your area and charge per hour.

3. Small Deliveries

Offer local delivery by walking/biking in your neighborhood for a fee.

4. WhatsApp Ads

Sell affordable products and promote via WhatsApp Status to your contact list.

5. Print & Type Services

Use public Wi-Fi or a small printer to offer CV printing or typing at low prices.

Tips for Staying Consistent

  • Start small — R10 a day is better than zero.
  • Use a journal or app to track your earnings and goals.
  • Stick to your time blocks — hustle for 1 hour every evening.
  • Ignore noise — not everyone needs to understand your vision.

Helpful Tools & Apps

  • Trello: Track side hustles and weekly progress
  • SnapScan / Capitec App: Get paid easily
  • Canva: Design your hustles’ flyers for free
